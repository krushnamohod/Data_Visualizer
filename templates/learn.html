Data Visualization Guide

Understand the types of plots and their uses

Bar Chart

A bar chart is used to compare categorical data.

  • Columns: Categorical and Numeric
  • Use: Comparing categories, identifying trends

Line Plot

A line plot is used to show trends over time.

  • Columns: Date/Time and Numeric
  • Use: Observing trends, identifying patterns

Scatter Plot

A scatter plot is used to analyze relationships between two numeric variables.

  • Columns: Two Numeric
  • Use: Identifying correlations, spotting outliers

Histogram

A histogram is used to visualize the distribution of a single numeric variable.

  • Columns: Numeric
  • Use: Understanding data distribution, spotting skewness

Pie Chart

A pie chart is used to represent proportions within a whole.

  • Columns: Categorical and Numeric
  • Use: Visualizing parts of a whole, displaying proportions
